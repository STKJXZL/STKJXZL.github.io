About me

  • Lei He, 1997, Bozhou, Anhui Province, Doctorate in Engineering.
  • As the first or corresponding author, I have accumulated a total of 9 accepted SCI/EI papers. Among these, one paper is published in a top-tier journal in the first district of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, two papers are in top-tier journals in the second district, three papers in the third district, one paper in the fourth district, and two papers in high-quality Chinese journals. Additionally, I have been granted one international invention patent, one domestic invention patent, and six utility model patents. I have led one scientific research project for graduate students in Anhui universities and two research and innovation fund projects at Anhui University of Science and Technology. As a key member of scientific research, I have participated in three major scientific projects in Anhui Province and three collaborative innovation projects among universities in the province. I presented a special report at the academic conference on the prevention and control of typical dynamic disasters in deep mining of Chinese and Australian coal mines. I have been invited to serve as a reviewer for journals such as IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems, Measurement Science and Technology, Engineering Research Express, and Space: Science & Technology.
  • Founded and served as vice president and president of the 3D Innovative Design Association at Anhui University of Science and Technology for two years. Organized 3D printing and CAD/SOLIDWORKS training sessions, involving over 100 participants individually and more than 1,000 participants cumulatively. Received accolades including the second national prize in the 9th mapping competition, the first national prize in the 10th mapping competition, the first prize in Anhui Province in the 8th mechanical innovation design competition, the excellent prize in the 3rd China Machinery Industry Outstanding Engineer Education Alliance “Star Cup” graduation design competition, the third national prize in the 16th “Challenge Cup” competition, the silver prize in Anhui Province in the 5th “Internet Plus” Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, and the third national prize in the 15th energy-saving and emission reduction competition.
